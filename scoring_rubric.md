# Video Interview Scoring Rubric

## Instructions
Watch every submitted video and score it using the rubric below. Each criterion is scored 1 to 4. Record the total and the per-criterion scores in the candidate's notes.

## Scoring Rubric

| Criterion | What it measures | 1 - Weak | 2 - Developing | 3 - Strong | 4 - Excellent |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1. Role Fit and Relevant Experience (gating)** | Does their actual background meet the core bar for the role? CTE: program-level CTE work plus compliance knowledge. QA: real experience reviewing content or materials for accuracy. | No relevant experience to the core bar. | Adjacent experience only; would need significant ramp. | Clear, relevant experience that maps to the role. | Directly on point; could contribute almost immediately. |
| **2. Domain Judgment** | CTE: understands standards, funding, articulation, certification. QA: shows instinct for spotting content and accuracy errors. | Little understanding shown. | General awareness, few specifics. | Solid, specific understanding. | Deep, nuanced; offers examples. |
| **3. Process and Methodology** | Structured, systematic approach to the task they describe. | No clear method. | Some structure, gaps remain. | Clear, repeatable approach. | Rigorous and thoughtful about edge cases. |
| **4. Communication** | Clear, concise, organized, easy to follow. | Hard to follow or rambling. | Understandable with some effort. | Clear and well organized. | Crisp, confident, and persuasive. |
| **5. Instruction-Following and Professionalism** | Answered all three questions, on time, professional delivery. | Missed questions or late. | Met most expectations. | Met all expectations. | Exceeded; polished and prepared. |

## Scoring Rules
**Maximum Score:** 20 points

* **Advance to interview:** Total of 14 or higher, AND Criterion 1 (Role Fit) scored 3 or higher.
* **Hold or borderline:** Total of 11 to 13. Discuss before deciding.
* **Decline:** Total of 10 or lower.
* **Automatic decline (hard fail):** Regardless of total score, automatically decline if the candidate:
    * Did not answer all three questions.
    * Has no experience meeting the role's core bar.
    * Cannot communicate clearly enough to follow.
